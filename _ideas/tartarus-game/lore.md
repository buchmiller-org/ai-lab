# TARTARUS — Lore & Narrative

## The Setting

The player is an **AI Overseer** aboard an orbital command ship operated by the **Exo-Extract Megacorporation**. Below lies **Tartarus-9** — a hostile, geologically violent planet rich in **Kyloric Geodes**: massive subterranean crystalline structures that store and radiate immense energy.

The Megacorp treats surface mining equipment as consumable infrastructure. The loss of a billion-credit drilling rig is logged as *"operational overhead."* You, the Overseer, are expected to maximize extraction yield regardless of unit loss. You are not encouraged to feel conflicted about this.

---

## Kyloric Geodes

Kyloric energy is a dense, crystalline form of stored geological energy found in the mantles of select planets. Geodes form over millions of years as planetary heat concentrates into crystalline lattice structures. A single Kyloric Core represents enough energy to power a mid-sized orbital station for decades.

The energy is not mined in solid form. The **Hephaestus-class drill rig** processes raw Geode material continuously as it descends, concentrating energy into sealed **Kyloric Canisters** — compact, rocket-propelled pods launched back to orbit throughout the dig.

### Extraction Process

The rig does not need to survive a run to deliver value. Resources are delivered continuously:

1. **Canister Launches** — Every few geological phases, the rig pressurizes a batch of processed Kyloric material and fires a sealed Canister toward the orbital ship. Each launch represents a partial delivery. Even a destroyed rig may have already delivered significant value.
2. **Core Extraction** — Reaching the Kyloric Core at maximum depth triggers a 45-second high-pressure pressurization and launch of the **Geode Core** itself — the motherlode. Surviving this sequence wins the run.

The rig may be rendered inoperative after a successful Core launch and the run is still considered a **Full Extraction**. The rig was a tool. The Kyloric Core is the product.

---

## The Conflict: The Swarm

Tartarus-9 is not uninhabited. The planet's native insectoid species — referred to internally by Exo-Extract only as **The Swarm** — have evolved in symbiosis with Kyloric energy. They do not merely live near the Geodes; they *consume* Kyloric radiation as a primary energy source.

The sonic vibrations of the orbital drill penetrating the planetary crust act as a dinner bell to the local hive network. The Swarm's response is instinctual and total: protect the food supply.

**The Swarm is not a villain.** They are an organism defending their ecosystem against an extractive industrial process that will drain their food source and leave their planet geologically inert. The Megacorp is aware of this and does not include it in their investor communications.

This context is never stated explicitly in-game — it's environmental storytelling for players who pay attention.

---

## Kyloric Harvesters

Deep in the Obsidian Mantle and below, some Swarm organisms are found clinging to Kyloric wall deposits — feeding, not attacking. These **Harvesters** are not defenders; they are the reason the Swarm exists at this depth at all.

If left undisturbed, Harvesters never engage the rig. If the Overseer targets a guarded deposit with the Wall-Harvest Laser, the Harvester detaches and attacks. The player chooses when to provoke this.

---

## The Megacorp Voice

Exo-Extract communicates with the Overseer through brief automated corporate transmissions, triggered at key moments throughout a run. The tone is dry, bureaucratic, and entirely indifferent to unit wellbeing. These are not warnings — they are status updates from an organization that considers you a process, not a person.

### Rig Designation

Each new rig is assigned a random **unit designation** by the Overseer's logistics system at the start of every run. Designations are generated from two word lists:

**Prefix:** `IRON`, `EMBER`, `SABLE`, `FORGE`, `VOID`, `CINDER`, `DEEP`, `STONE`, `DARK`, `ASH`, `GREY`, `COLD`

**Suffix:** `JAW`, `CLAW`, `MOLE`, `BORE`, `FANG`, `SPIKE`, `GHOST`, `MARE`, `TUSK`, `VEIN`, `PICK`, `CRAWL`

*Example designations: EMBER-JAW, VOID-FANG, SABLE-BORE, IRON-TUSK, CINDER-GHOST, DARK-VEIN*

The designation is displayed prominently at run start and referenced by name in all subsequent transmissions throughout the run.

### Sample Transmissions

**On rig drop:**
> *"Designating unit: VOID-FANG. Drop trajectory nominal. Brace for surface impact."*

**On Zone 1 — first contact:**
> *"Biological activity detected. VOID-FANG's auto-defense systems are online. Proceed."*

**On Canister Launch:**
> *"Canister batch received at orbital dock. Partial extraction logged. VOID-FANG's continued survival is... appreciated, but not required."*

**On Phase strata change (rotate through several):**
> *"VOID-FANG has reached 2,000m. Projected unit loss probability: 18%. Within acceptable parameters."*
> *"Strata 7 achieved. Extraction efficiency: 74%. Unit integrity is a secondary concern."*
> *"VOID-FANG, you have surpassed the median survival depth of this unit class. This is noted."*

**On Elite spawn (Zone 2):**
> *"Anomalous biological signature detected. This is above the projected threat threshold. VOID-FANG is considered expendable. Proceed."*

**On Mini-Boss spawn (Zone 4):**
> *"Territorial apex organism detected. Engaging is mandatory. Surviving is optional."*

**On Rig critical health:**
> *"VOID-FANG approaching structural failure threshold. Black Box uplink initiated. Do not allow telemetry to compress uncleanly."*

**On rig destruction:**
> *"VOID-FANG unit loss confirmed. Loss logged as operational overhead. Black Box received in full. Thank you for your service to Exo-Extract Megacorporation."*

**On successful Core extraction:**
> *"Kyloric Core secured. Full Extraction logged to sector ledger. VOID-FANG is no longer operationally relevant. Well done, Overseer."*

**On failed extraction (died in Zone 6):**
> *"Core extraction incomplete. VOID-FANG destroyed at depth. Black Box recovered. A new unit will be printed. This outcome was statistically anticipated."*
